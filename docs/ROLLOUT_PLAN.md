# Rollout Plan — Launch of the Global Hall of Honor

**Date**: 2026-07-06
**Seats**: Architecture · Frontend · Backend · DevOps · Payments · Trust & Safety ·
Growth · **Marketing (lead voice)** · **Gamification (new seat)**
**Launch goal**: 100+ submissions on day one. Seed 50 honorees before doors open.

---

## 1. The Listing Threshold — 10 co-signs (decision + reasoning)

**Decision: 10 to be listed. Not more. Here's the math the room agreed on:**

- A motivated family can rally 10 co-signs in an afternoon (group chat + church/station/
  class network). 25+ would strand submissions in limbo during launch week when the
  network is empty — dead pages kill morale and referrals.
- 10 co-signs × 100 submissions = **1,000+ new verified-email accounts on day one**.
  The threshold IS the growth engine.
- Prestige above the bar is already handled by the ladder (50/100/1K). The listing
  threshold is a floor, not a status — keep it universally achievable.

**Mechanics (reconciles with VERIFICATION_MODEL.md):**
- On submission, the page goes live immediately at an **unlisted URL** — shareable, so
  people can reach it to co-sign ("private until witnessed")
- At **10 co-signs → LISTED in the Halls** (discover, search, map, category pages) +
  celebration moment (confetti, badge, auto-generated "Now Listed" share card)
- Progress bar on every pending page: "7 of 10 witnesses — 3 more to enter the Halls,"
  with co-signer names/avatars shown (social proof + rally pressure)
- Posthumous/historical submissions: same 10, but require Tier 1 identity on the
  *submitter* (stolen-valor and impersonation guard)

## 2. Votes & Objections — honor culture, not Reddit

**Decision: up-votes only. No public negativity anywhere on an honoree page.**

- **Up-votes** (distinct from co-signs): any account can applaud a story; co-signs are
  the weightier "I witness this legacy" act with a relationship tag. Applause is
  unlimited; co-signs are one per person per honoree.
- **Objections are private, structured, and reviewed** — never a public counter:
  - "Raise a concern" link (footer of every profile, deliberately quiet)
  - Categories: factual inaccuracy · impersonation · stolen valor · inappropriate
    content · "I am this person and did not consent"
  - Requires an account + written reason; rate-limited (3 open objections per account)
  - Goes to Trust & Safety review queue; 72-hour SLA; every action audit-logged
  - Outcomes: dismiss / request correction from family / unlist pending evidence /
    remove + revoke badges (fraud)
  - Objector never sees who co-signed; family never sees who objected (both directions
    of retaliation blocked)
- Consent path: honorees can claim their own page (identity verification) and gain
  edit/takedown rights — living honorees always outrank their submitters

## 3. The Launch Offer — "Founding Honorees" (limited-time free)

- **Free submission ($19 → $0) for the first 30 days or first 1,000 honorees,
  whichever comes first** — both counters real and public (no fake scarcity)
- Hero Image ($29), Legacy Archive, and prints stay paid — revenue preserved, and the
  free cohort is the print-upsell audience
- Every free-window honoree gets a permanent **FOUNDING HONOREE** mark (laurel ring on
  the badge) — earned by earliness, not payment → passes the Marquis test
- After the window: price returns to $19, founding mark retires forever. That
  permanence is the urgency — not a countdown gimmick.

## 4. Seeding — 50 Charter Honorees (2 weeks pre-launch)

- Recruit 50 families/orgs in private beta: Chip's network first (Marshall HS Chicago
  alumni, house/gospel/stepper communities), plus 1–2 per category across
  police/fire/military/educators/healthcare/music/dance
- Each charter honoree enters launch day **already listed** (10+ co-signs gathered in
  beta) with hero image comped — the Halls must look alive, never empty
- Geographic spread on purpose: Chicago (home base), Atlanta, Houston, LA, NYC —
  five cities for the city-race mechanic and local press pitches
- Every charter family commits to one action: share their honoree at launch-day noon
  (coordinated wave)

## 5. Marketing Strategy (expert seat)

**Positioning line for launch**: *"Wikipedia has rules about who matters. We don't."*
(backed by the anti-Marquis trust story: verification free forever, no sales calls)

**Channel plan, in priority order:**
1. **Charter-family wave** — 50 families × their networks is the day-one engine;
   each share lands on an unlisted-or-listed page with a co-sign CTA
2. **Waitlist with referral ladder** (opens 2 weeks out): position improves per
   referral; top-100 waitlist get founding submission slots reserved.
   Target: 2,000 emails → 3–5% day-one submission = 60–100 organic + 50 seeded ✓
3. **Community-targeted outreach**: police/fire union locals, alumni associations,
   HBCU + Divine Nine networks, gospel choirs, Chicago steppers groups — one warm
   intro each, not spray-and-pray
4. **Local press, 5 markets**: hometown-hero stories are local-news oxygen; pitch is
   the charter honoree in that city, not the platform
5. **Reveal-moment content**: TikTok/IG reels of families seeing the hero image for
   the first time — the emotional peak is the ad. Charter beta produces the first
   10 reels before launch.
6. **Veteran/first-responder creators**: 3–5 mid-tier creators gifted founding
   submissions for their own honorees (disclosed, never paid-for-praise)

**Day-one choreography**: 9am waitlist doors open → noon charter wave → 6pm
"first 100 Founding Honorees" wall goes live → 9pm day-one recap share card.

## 6. Gamification (new seat) — launch mechanics

- **Rally bar**: 7/10 progress with witness avatars on every pending page (the single
  highest-leverage mechanic — it recruits FOR us)
- **Founding Wall**: permanent "First 100" monument page; slots visibly filling all day
- **City race**: live counter — Chicago vs Atlanta vs Houston vs LA vs NYC submissions;
  local pride is free distribution
- **Honor Number claims** at every co-sign ("You're now 1° from a listed honoree")
- **Milestone confetti + auto share cards** at 10 (Listed), 50, 100 co-signs
- **Anti-dark-pattern rules** (T&S co-signed): all counters real; no fake urgency; no
  guilt notifications; no streaks; celebration, never shame

## 8. Launch-day KPIs

- **100+ submissions** (primary) · 1,000+ accounts via co-signs
- ≥60% of day-one submissions reach Listed (10 co-signs) within 72h
- Share rate ≥40% of listed pages · Hero-image attach ≥20% of free submissions
- Objections < 2% of listings, all resolved inside SLA

## 9. Risks & counters

- **Co-sign fraud rings** (10 fake accounts to get listed): velocity caps, device/IP
  clustering, email-domain diversity heuristic; suspicious listings quietly re-queued
- **Objection brigading**: rate limits + account-age requirement + dedupe per profile
- **Free-window abuse** (spam submissions): attestation + submitter email verification
  + the 10-co-sign bar itself (spam can't rally 10 real witnesses)
- **Seeded-content optics**: charter honorees are real people with consenting families,
  labeled Founding like everyone else — nothing astroturfed
